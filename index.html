The Mission

"Youth-led, in-person game jam where creators design playable games that challenge stereotypes, empower young voices, and imagine inclusive futures."

We are building a platform for young creators to integrate STEM concepts with social inclusion. Powered by Revivals Egypt, this event builds on a legacy of youth-led community & culture projects, including climate action and equality initiatives.

Why Re:Play?

  • ✓ Youth-Led: Your culture make the decisions.
  • ✓ In-Person: Real collaboration in Alexandria.
  • ✓ Social Impact: Games that mean something.
  • ✓ Global: Connected to international networks.

Why Support Re:Play?

Re:Play Alexandria is independently organized to remain free and accessible for youth in Egypt.

  • Free participation for young creators
  • Safe in-person collaboration space
  • Food, internet access, and equipment
  • Mentorship and judging support

Every contribution goes directly into making youth-led creation possible in a context where access to resources is limited.

"Re:Play Alexandria is organized by a local youth team with a track record of mobilizing independent, high-engagement events in Alexandria."
